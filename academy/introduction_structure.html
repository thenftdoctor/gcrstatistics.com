Introduction

How the course is structured


The general structure
The course contains 5 different sections that cover a broad topic, such as ‘the basics’, ‘estimating and testing’, ‘regression models’ and ‘study design’. Each of these sections consists of multiple lessons, which form the cornerstone of this course. Lesson 7 & 8 contain multiple subparts due to their comprehensive nature, and bonus lesson 10 is a little gift from me.

The structure of a lesson
At the heart of each lesson is a video presentation. Just click on it to view the video (don’t forget to turn on the audio) and view it in full-screen modus. The videos are recorded in high definition, but the quality will automatically adjust to your internet speed. If it is a little unsharp try setting it at 1080p with the button on the bottom right corner.

Enough talking.. Get started, and have fun!
